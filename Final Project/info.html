How you can help

if you are concerned about a loved one, there are things you can do to help:

  • Let them know you are there to listen without judgement
  • Try to keep in touch. A simple text message or call can make the world of difference
  • Find out about local serivces or support groups
  • Help them seek help. Reassure them that it is okay to reach out for help.
